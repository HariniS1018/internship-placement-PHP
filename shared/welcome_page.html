INTERNSHIPS

CUIC facilitates internships ( with stipend) for the UG / PG students of the University Departments: 6 months internships for UG / PG students in their final semesters & summer internships for pre-final UG students. On the successful completion of the internship, the internship offer may get converted into a full time offer (pre-placement offer (PPO)), if the industry desires so.

PLACEMENTS

CUIC is committed to facilitate placement opportunities for final year UG/PG students of the University Departments (College of Engineering, Guindy (CEG), Madras Institute of Technology, Chrompet (MIT)/ Alagappa College of Technology (ACT) / School of Architecture and Planning (SAP))
